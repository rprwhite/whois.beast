Beer. Coffee. Fungi.

A modern farmhouse in this connected age of concrete and tarmac.
The Sophisticated Beast brews dry, but not too dry, fun, seasonal ales for the evenings and sultry, revitalising, nitro coffee for the mornings. The sought of brews that are sought after and fought over, cheered and jeered, and always something special.
What about the fungi? Well, we also hand farm and harvest a menagerie of mushrooms, no boring buttons. Grown with pleasure, and an eye to sustainability, on the spent brewers grain and coffee grinds.

The Sophisticated Beast is seriously small and the journey from garage to our new home has only just begun.
